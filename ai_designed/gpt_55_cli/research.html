How fine-tuning data changes reasoning after RL

Reasoning Through Chess: How Reasoning Evolves from Data Through Fine-Tuning and Reinforcement Learning

We fine-tuned a 7B language model on chess data, then used reinforcement learning to study what changed: move quality, hallucination, and whether the model's reasoning matched the move it chose.

arXiv PDF
  • Best-move SFT produced the strongest post-RL chess performance.
  • Best-line SFT produced comparable performance with more faithful reasoning and more stable RL.
  • RL reduced hallucinations as a side effect while shifting move quality upward.
  • SFT checkpoint metrics predicted post-RL model performance.
