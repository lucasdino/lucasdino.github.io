RL for language models — and the data that shapes them.

I'm working on reinforcement learning for LLMs with the PEARLS Lab at UCSD.

In progress

The current project is a corporate simulation used as a multi-turn RL environment for LLMs. The goal: simulate market dynamics with semi-optimal competition, so we can generate realistic tasks in finance, data analysis, and general corporate strategy — the kind that require multi-turn interaction and tool use, with real costs.

NeurIPS '25
Lead Author

Reasoning Through Chess: How Reasoning Evolves from Data Through Fine-Tuning and Reinforcement Learning

Lucas Dionisopoulos et al.

We outperform state-of-the-art open-source reasoning models in chess through SFT and RL on a 7B-parameter language model. The focus of the work is how fine-tuning data influences post-RL reasoning — both quantitative performance and the qualitative shape of the model's thoughts — using custom, theoretically-inspired datasets.

Currently under review for ICML 2026.
